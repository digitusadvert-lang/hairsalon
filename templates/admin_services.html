How Service Durations Work

Each service has a specific duration that determines how appointment slots are booked:

  • Haircut (45min): Books a 45-minute slot
  • Color Treatment (150min): Books a 2.5 hour slot
  • Services with appointments cannot be deleted - they will be deactivated instead
  • Deactivated services remain in the system for historical data but aren't available for new bookings
